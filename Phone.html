How Does the Breakthrough Technology Works?

Hughesnet® Voice service operates through a cutting-edge technology known as Hughesnet® Voice-over-IP (VoIP). This innovative system communicates over the same satellite connection employed by your Hughesnet® satellite Internet service. During your installation session, a Hughesnet® -certified professional will set up a device called an ATA (analog telephone adapter). This device seamlessly routes call through your modem and satellite dish. It’s a straightforward process.
